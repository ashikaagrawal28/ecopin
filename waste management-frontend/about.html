About Us

"A cleaner community starts with you."
By the Waste Management System, we believe in empowering individuals and communities to take control of their environment. By providing a platform where you can easily report unattended waste, we make it simple for everyone to contribute to a cleaner, healthier neighborhood.

How It Works:

  • Report Waste: Spot unattended waste? Use our interactive map to pin the location, describe the issue, and upload photos.
  • Track Progress: After submitting your report, our team will review it and take action. You’ll receive updates along the way.
  • Get Rewarded: Receive points for every valid report you make. Be part of a solution that benefits both you and the environment.
